My Work

I’ve been building various websites for over three years now, and I’ve come a long way from my very first website – a wedding website for my now-wife and I. This section of the portfolio is designed to showcase my best work, which is mostly the things that I’ve made most recently. However, since I’ve worked in a breadth of languages, some progression in my learning should become apparent through various projects undertaken at different times. While I may not have five years of experience or entire libraries to showcase, the fact that I have a mostly non-school related portfolio of websites that I’ve built on my own time is something that I’m proud of.

As a side note: all of my code can be found in one of my publicly-hosted GitHub repositories. I'll include links to these in each individual project page.

Blog

Yeah, if I were you, I would probably not read any of these posts. Seriously, I have Google Analytics turned on, so I'll know if you did, and I'll just feel bad that someone made such a poor mistake. Just let me write terrible things in peace, please.
